Common Questions

Things People Ask Us

How long does a typical project take?
▾
It depends on the scope. A simple website takes 1–2 weeks. A full-stack web app usually takes 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline in our proposal.
Do you work with clients outside India?
▾
Yes! We work with clients globally. All communication is done online via email, WhatsApp, or video calls. Time zones are never a problem for us.
What information do you need to get started?
▾
Just your idea! Tell us what you want to build, who it's for, and your approximate budget. We'll take it from there and ask the right questions.
Can I request changes after the project is done?
▾
Absolutely. All our packages include a revision round. For larger changes post-launch, we offer affordable maintenance plans so you're always covered.
Do you sign NDAs or confidentiality agreements?
▾
Yes, we respect your privacy and IP. We're happy to sign an NDA before any project discussion if you require it. Just mention it when you reach out.
What payment methods do you accept?
▾
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and PayPal for international clients. We usually take 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery for new clients.
