LawSoft — My City Lawyer

A MERN stack + WebRTC platform for lawyer search and real-time consultation.

LawSoft is our most ambitious project to date — a full-stack web platform that allows users to search for lawyers by specialty, location, and availability. Built with the MERN stack and WebRTC for real-time video consultations, it was developed as a 5-member team project under the guidance of Prof. Sumit Kumar Sinha at B.P. Poddar Institute.
