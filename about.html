Our Vision

Our Manifesto
We started with zero funding, one shared laptop, and an unshakeable belief that great code is the most powerful tool in the world.

New Day was not born in a fancy office or an accelerator program. It was born in a college classroom, between lectures, during late-night coding sessions, and over countless cups of chai. Five BCA students from B.P. Poddar Institute looked at the world and said — we can build something better.

We don't believe in waiting until we're "ready." We believe in learning by doing, shipping fast, and improving constantly. Every bug we fix makes us sharper. Every project we complete makes us hungrier. We are students by morning and builders by night.

Our stack is modern, our ideas are ambitious, and our commitment is absolute. New Day is not just a company name — it is a promise. Every single day, we wake up and choose to build something that matters.

Our Journey

From Idea to Reality

How we got here — and where we're going
2023 — The Spark
Team Formation at B.P. Poddar
Five BCA students with different skills but one shared dream — to build something real. The idea of New Day Software was born in a college lab.
Founding Moment
2024 — First Build
LawSoft / My City Lawyer
Our first major project — a MERN stack + WebRTC platform for lawyer search, built under Prof. Sumit Kumar Sinha. We learned what it means to build a real product under pressure.
MERN + WebRTC
2025 — Going Deeper
Nova AI & Skill Expansion
Work began on Nova AI — a Python-powered personal voice assistant. The team deepened skills in Python, AI integration, SQL, and Java for future opportunities.
Python · AI · Voice
2025 — Project New Day
Company Website Launch
The website you're reading right now. A commitment to put our name on the internet and make it official. New Day Software is open for business.
HTML · CSS · JS
2026 — The Future
Full Software Company Launch
After graduation, the team plans to officially register New Day as a software company — taking on real clients, building real products, and changing lives through technology.
